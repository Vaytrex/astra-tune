ASTRA-TUNE

Real-time pitch corrector — from transparent tuning to the classic hard-tune vocal effect.

This installer places the plugin where your audio software will find it:

  • AAX → Pro Tools (if included in this build)
  • Audio Unit → Logic Pro, GarageBand
  • VST3 → Ableton Live, Cubase, Reaper, FL Studio
  • Standalone app → /Applications

On the next screens you can choose which formats to install. After installing, restart your DAW and rescan plugins if it doesn't appear.
