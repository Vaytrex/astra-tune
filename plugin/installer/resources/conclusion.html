ASTRA-TUNE is installed

Quick start:

  • Insert ASTRA-TUNE on a vocal track (Effects → Pitch Shift category in most hosts).
  • Set the Key and Scale of your song.
  • Retune Speed at 0 ms gives the hard robotic effect; 100–400 ms gives natural, transparent correction.
  • Use headphones when monitoring live input.

If the plugin doesn't appear, restart your DAW and trigger a plugin rescan.
